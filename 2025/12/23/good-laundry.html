A good experience on a bad day

(December 23, 2025)
Tags: moments; joy;

Today has been rough.

I’m not sure if there’s something in the air but today has been pretty crappy. I spent about 4x the amount of time I should’ve putting together a bed frame after wasting my morning. Then I realized the bed frame doesn’t account for my mattress’ box spring so now I have to figure out how to dispose of that. Then I took an accidental nap and wasted another few hours being overstimulated.

But after a while things started to turn around. I got to do some laundry at a new laundromat, that was nice. I got to enjoy some peace and quiet in that laundromat. And randomly I figured out a fix for some old not working bluetooth earbuds so they’re working again! Today was bad, now it’s not so bad. I’m grateful.

Per audacia ad astra, thanks for reading.

A refresh to my website

(December 14, 2025)
Status: Complete
Categories: project;
Tags: web-dev;

My website has been refreshed, I’ve added a bunch of new features and revamped some old ones.

  1. Added an open source/attributions page.
  2. Cleaned up my homepage.
  3. Updated the posts logic to now have pagination and autopages for each category and tag on the site.
  4. Finally started using includes and cleaned up some of the posts/metadata logic for posts and pages.
  5. Deleted some old redundant pages.
  6. Changed my website’s main font to finally be good and readable.
  7. Improved mobile support and formatting.
  8. Consolidated my RSS/Atom feeds.
  9. Updated my backend workflow to support Jekyll 4.4.1 and plugins.
  10. Actually put stuff on my about page!

And probably some other stuff I forgot to mention. Most of this stuff has been on the backburner for a long time, I’m really happy to finally go through with it!

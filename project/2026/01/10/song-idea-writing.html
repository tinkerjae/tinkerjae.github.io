Working on a process for writing song ideas

(January 10, 2026)
Categories: project;
Tags: music;

Hey guys! Welcome to 2026!

This one’s gotta be another quick one but I’ve been working on this as of late. I often struggle with writing down song ideas as I have them, and this makes the process of music creation really difficult. To combat this, I’ve got a little method of writing down ideas now.

Basic Song Drafting:

  1. Rhythm + Tempo
  2. Melody + Harmony
  3. Song Structure

Intermediate Song Drafting:

  1. Timbre + Texture
  2. Dynamics + Articulation
  3. Style/Vibe

Advanced Song Drafting:

  1. Concept/Story/Narrative
  2. Song Write Up
  3. Lyrics if wanted

I’ll see how well this works and hopefully will do more with this soon. Bye for now!
